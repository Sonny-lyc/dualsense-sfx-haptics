This folder bundles two third-party components, mirrored here with permission per
their published licenses (see PROJECT_CHANGES.md / README "Third-Party" section
for the original research):

1. Mod Engine (loader)
   Author: Katalash
   Source: https://www.nexusmods.com/sekiro/mods/6
   License note: "You are free to distribute and UNMODIFIED ModEngine WITH a mod"
   dinput8.dll is redistributed unmodified.

2. PS5 Glyphs (button-prompt texture swap)
   Source: https://www.nexusmods.com/sekiro/mods/2113
   License note: Permissions explicitly allow re-upload / repackaging into other
   projects with attribution; not for paid/commercial redistribution.
   mods/menu/hi/01_common.tpf.dcx is redistributed unmodified.

Neither component is required for the haptics feature to work. Installing them only
swaps Sekiro's on-screen button-prompt icons from Xbox to PS5 style (static texture
replacement — Steam Input hides real controller identity from the game, so this is
not "detection", it always shows PS5-style icons regardless of what's plugged in).
